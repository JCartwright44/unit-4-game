Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your current score.

You win the game by matching your current score to the answer number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts again, the game will change the values of each crystal.
